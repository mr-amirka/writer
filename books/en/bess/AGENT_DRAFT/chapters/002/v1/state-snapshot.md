## Characters

### Bess
- **Location:** Kitchen doorway, then sitting in the doorway watching Anna on the floor.
- **Emotional state:** Still waiting. Registers the new smell as significant. Does not connect it to Henry's absence. Eats when the bowl is filled.
- **Active goals:** The bowl. The range. Tom in the morning. The routine continuing in some form.
- **What changed in this chapter:** A new smell has entered the house. The woman feeds her at the end of the day. Bess accepts the food.
- **Relationships:** Anna is not Tom. Not Henry. Bess watches her carefully. Ends the chapter in the same room as her.

### Anna
- **Status:** Arrived from Leeds. First day at the farm. Solicitor. Efficient by training but at a loss here.
- **Location:** Kitchen floor, back against the cupboards, end of day.
- **What changed:** Arrived. Went through the house. Found the tin. Sat on the floor. Did not cry (this chapter).
- **Relationships:** Tom — polite but brief. Bess — looked at each other. Anna fed her. That is all.

### Tom
- **Location:** Came and went. Lives half a mile down the lane.
- **What changed:** Brief meeting with Anna in the kitchen. He and Anna spoke — carefully. He left.
- **Will Tom keep coming:** Not resolved. He left before it could be asked.

### Henry
- **Status:** Gone. His smell on the flat cap, thinning.
- **Objects remaining:** Flat cap (on the hook). The range (now lit by Tom). Tools in the yard.
