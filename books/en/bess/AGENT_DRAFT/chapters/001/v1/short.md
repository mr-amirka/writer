# Chapter 001 — Summary ✦ v1

## What Happened

Before dawn, Bess wakes at her usual station at the foot of the stairs and waits for Henry. He doesn't come down. She waits through the changing light — grey to pale gold — understanding something is wrong through smell before anything else. She goes upstairs (unusual — she almost never does), pushes open the bedroom door, finds Henry in his bed. She lies down beside him. Tom arrives at seven-fifteen, lets himself in, calls Henry's name, sees Bess at the top of the stairs. He goes up. Later, people arrive. Bess retreats to the kitchen, to the unlit range, beneath Henry's flat cap on its hook.

## Key Changes

- Henry has died (in bed, during the night)
- Bess is alone in the farmhouse for the first time
- The reader understands everything; Bess understands only that something has changed

## Bess's State at End

**Location:** Kitchen, beneath the unlit range
**Emotional state:** Waiting. Not distressed in the human sense — registering the absence of normal.
**Active goals:** Wait for the morning routine to resume.

## What's Unresolved

- Who will feed Bess and manage the sheep?
- What will happen to the farm?
- Tom knows now — what does he do next?
