# State Snapshot — Chapter 001, Variant v1

**Covers events:** Pre-dawn → afternoon of one day (late October)
**Approved variant:** yes (author selected v1)

---

## Characters

### Bess
- **Location:** Farmhouse kitchen, beneath the unlit range.
- **Emotional state:** Waiting. Does not understand what happened. Registers absence of normal; does not register loss.
- **Active goals:** Wait for the morning routine to resume. Wait for someone to fill the bowl.
- **What changed in this chapter:** Lost the only person she knows. Does not know this.
- **Relationships:** Henry is physically gone. In Bess's understanding — left and hasn't come back (yet).

### Henry
- **Status:** Dead. Body has been taken.
- **What remains:** Smell (fading). The flat cap on the hook. His tools in the yard. The range cold for the first time.

### Tom
- **Role in chapter:** Found Henry. Made the calls. Was the last to leave.
- **Status:** Lives half a mile down the lane. Has been working here twenty-two years. Will come back tomorrow.

---

## Artefacts and Key Objects

### The Farmhouse
- **Status:** Physically unchanged. Legally: Henry's estate. Beneficiary is Anna.
- **What changed:** Became empty in the human sense. For Bess — became different.

### The Flat Cap
- **Status:** Still on the hook by the kitchen door. Henry's.
- **Significance:** Recurring image. Chapter ends with it.

### The Range
- **Status:** Cold. Unlit. Henry always lit it at five-thirty.
- **Significance:** The first physical sign that the routine has broken.

## World Facts Established in This Chapter

- Henry Moorhouse died during the night (heart, in his sleep)
- Tom has his own key to the farmhouse (keeps it for emergencies)
- The farm has sheep in the fields — they still need tending
- Henry never left the range to go cold overnight
