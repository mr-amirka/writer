## Appearance

Dark hair, cut short. Wears things that travel well. She looks like someone who is passing through — because she thinks she is.

## Character

**Public mask:** Competence. "I'll sort it." She does sort things. She's good at her job. She applies this to the farm as if it were a case.

**True nature:** She can care deeply. She doesn't know how to admit she's caring.

**Main fear:** Belonging to something she can't control.

**Main desire:** A place that is genuinely hers — not her father's, not her ex-husband's. Hers.

**Main lie she tells herself:** "I'm just here to sort out the estate."

## Speech

Direct. Short sentences. She says what she means and expects others to do the same. Henry was like this too, she realises, and doesn't know what to do with that.
