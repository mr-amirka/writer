# Anna — Author's Notes

*Raw character sketch.*

---

Anna Moorhouse. 35. Solicitor, Leeds. Divorced two years ago.

Grew up on the farm. Left at seventeen for university. Never really came back — Christmas once, Easter once, maybe. She had her reasons. She doesn't talk about them.

She's good at her job. Organised. Clear. She deals with things. That's how she thinks of herself: someone who deals with things.

She didn't know Henry was ill. He didn't tell her. She didn't know because she didn't ask. That's the thing she can't quite get past.

She comes to sort out the estate. She expects it to take two weeks. It takes longer.

---

The farm: she loved it as a child. She also couldn't wait to leave it. Both things are true.

Bess: Anna doesn't know dogs. Or thinks she doesn't. She comes in on the second day and finds Bess in the kitchen, and she stands there for a while, and then she gets a tin from the pantry and opens it.

She doesn't know why she does that.
