# Tom — Author's Notes

*Raw character sketch.*

---

Tom Greenwood. 58. Farmhand. Has worked at the Moorhouse farm for twenty-two years.

He's not a talkative man. He and Henry worked together without much talking. They knew what needed doing. He'd arrive, they'd do it, he'd go.

He has a wife, a terrier, and a bungalow half a mile down the lane. He doesn't have any plans that don't involve this farm.

He doesn't know what to do now. So he keeps coming.

That's all there is to say about Tom, for now.
