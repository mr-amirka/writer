# Chapter 002 — Author Notes (raw)

*Written: 2026-06-06*

---

Two weeks have passed. Tom has been coming every day — the sheep still need tending, and Bess needs feeding. He lights the range. He doesn't say much.

Anna arrives. She's coming from Leeds to deal with the estate — she's a solicitor so she understands the legal side but not the farming side or the animal side. She's efficient. She has a list.

She comes in through the front — she doesn't know that no one uses the front. That's important somehow.

Tom is there when she arrives or comes back while she's there. Brief exchange. He's polite but not warm. Then he leaves.

Anna goes through the house. She doesn't know where things are. She stands in doors.

The flat cap is still there. She looks at it.

Bess watches from doorways.

In the evening Anna is hungry and realises Bess hasn't been fed yet. Or has she? Maybe she hasn't, because Tom left. She finds the tin in the pantry but doesn't know where the tin opener is.

She finds it eventually. Opens the tin. Puts the bowl down. Sits on the floor. 

That's the ending — Anna sitting on the kitchen floor with Bess watching from the doorway. Don't explain why she sits on the floor. She just does.
