## Before Writing Any Chapter

1. Read `APPROVED/chapters/{N-1}.short.md` — what happened before.
2. Read `state-snapshot.md` of the last approved chapter.
3. Read `APPROVED/universe/characters/bess.md` — protagonist's current state.
4. Read `APPROVED/universe/world.md` and `lore.md` — the rules.
5. Read `PLAN.md` — what should happen in this chapter.

## Core Rules for This Book

- Bess does not understand death. Never show her "realising" Henry is gone.
- Bess's perception is always sensory: smell → sound → temperature → routine.
- No sentimentality. The emotion lives in what is not said.
- Every chapter ends on a concrete physical image, not an emotional statement.
- Dialogue is minimal. What's left unsaid matters more.
- The flat cap on the hook is the recurring anchor. Handle with care.

---

## Minimum 3 Variants

Always generate v1, v2, v3 for each chapter or major universe element.
Variants must differ meaningfully in approach — pace, structure, PoV distance — not in minor wording.
