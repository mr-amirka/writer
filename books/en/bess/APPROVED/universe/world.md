## Overview

A stone farmhouse in the Yorkshire Dales, built in the 1840s. Three fields: bottom field, top field, the long field along the beck. Dry stone walls. October. The light goes at four.

The house has been in the Moorhouse family since Henry's father. Henry never left it. It is the kind of house you are from.

## Atmosphere

The house smells of decades: stone, woodsmoke, lanolin, pipe tobacco. October mornings: fog in the bottom of the valley, the sheep appearing out of it. Cold that doesn't apologise.

## What Makes This World Particular

The farm doesn't know Henry is gone. The sheep are in the field. The range needs lighting at five-thirty. These things continue.
