# Chapter 001 — Final Summary ✦ APPROVED

## What Happened

Before dawn, Bess wakes at her station at the foot of the stairs and waits for Henry. He doesn't come down. She waits through the changing light — dark to grey to pale gold — registering that something is wrong through smell before any other sense. She goes upstairs and finds Henry in bed, cold. She lies beside him. Tom arrives at seven-fifteen, lets himself in, calls Henry's name once, sees Bess at the top of the stairs. He goes up. Later people arrive. Bess retreats to the kitchen, to the place beside the unlit range, beneath Henry's flat cap on its hook. She doesn't move until dark.

## Key Changes

- Henry has died (in his sleep, during the night)
- Bess is alone in the farmhouse for the first time
- The reader understands everything; Bess understands only that something has changed

## Bess's State at End

**Location:** Kitchen, beneath the unlit range, under Henry's flat cap.
**Emotional state:** Waiting. Registering the absence of normal; not registering loss in the human sense.
**Active goals:** Wait for the morning routine to resume.

## What's Unresolved

- Who will tend the sheep and feed Bess?
- What will happen to the farm?
- Tom knows now — what does he do?
