## Chapter-by-Chapter Narrative Plan

### Chapter 001 — "The Morning He Didn't Come Down"
**Scene:** The farmhouse, before dawn
**PoV:** Bess
**Chapter goal:** Introduce the world through a dog's perception. Henry has died. The reader understands before Bess does.
**Character goal:** Bess waits for the morning routine. It doesn't come.
**Conflict:** Bess cannot process what happened — only registers the absence of normal
**Chapter ending:** Tom lets himself in through the back door. He calls Henry's name once.

---

### Chapter 002 — "New Smell"
**Scene:** The same farmhouse, two weeks later. The day Anna arrives.
**PoV:** Bess
**Chapter goal:** Introduce Anna and Tom. Establish the central tension: the dog, the farm, and a woman who doesn't know what she's doing there.
**Character goal:** Bess wants things to go back to normal. Or at least someone to open the back door.
**Conflict:** Anna wants to sort everything quickly. The farm — and Bess — won't sort.
**Chapter ending:** Anna opens a tin of something from the pantry and puts it in Bess's bowl. She sits on the kitchen floor. Bess watches from the doorway.
